About Rabbi's Scrolls

A blog built to preserve and proclaim truth.

Rabbi's Scrolls was created as a digital archive of biblical teachings, doctrinal insights, and pastoral reflections — a modern scroll for a new generation.

Inspired by the command to "guard the deposit entrusted to you" (1 Timothy 6:20), this site is designed to be a reliable, secure, and enduring resource for seekers and teachers alike.

It stands as a watchtower — not of human wisdom, but of divine truth — where clarity, order, and reverence guide every post.

The Vision

This blog exists to preserve and proclaim timeless truths in a digital age. Every article is written with the conviction that Scripture is "living and active" (Hebrews 4:12), and that the Word of God must be handled with care, precision, and reverence.

Technical Foundation

Behind the sacred words is a modern, secure, and scalable web application built with the following technologies:

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3 (custom parchment theme), JavaScript (ES6+), Bootstrap Icons
  • Backend: Spring Boot (Kotlin), Spring Security, Thymeleaf, JPA/Hibernate
  • Authentication: JWT (stateless), Role-Based Access Control (ADMIN only)
  • Database: PostgreSQL (relational, ACID-compliant)
  • Deployment: Embedded Tomcat, HTTPS-ready

Design & Aesthetics

The visual design draws from ancient parchment and candlelight — soft cream backgrounds, gold accents, serif typography (EB Garamond), and subtle gradients evoke a sense of reverence and timelessness.

Every UI element is hand-crafted to reflect the sacred nature of the content — no distractions, only clarity.
